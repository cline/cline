# Discovery Integration

## Purpose

Use this reference when `meta-knowledge` is called from a DISCOVERY sprint or from `aidlc-discovery-sprint`.

## Expected Inputs

Prefer to gather:

- the sprint brief
- the sprint review
- the Playwright summary
- the relevant audit entries
- the updated discovery artifact set
- the next sprint backlog

## Discovery-Specific Questions

Ask:

1. What slowed the sprint down?
2. Which discovery artifacts were missing or weak?
3. Did validation happen at the right time?
4. Did discovery findings reach INCEPTION soon enough?
5. Should `aidlc-discovery-sprint` change its loop, templates, or helper-skill sequencing?

## Common Outputs

Discovery-driven meta outputs often include:

- updates to `.agents/skills/aidlc-discovery-sprint/SKILL.md`
- updates to discovery templates
- updates to helper-skill sequencing
- updates to completion gates
- new notes in `aidlc-docs/meta-knowledge/knowledge-base.md`
- backlog items for future rule cleanup

## High-Value Signal

Treat these as strong candidates for improvement:

- repeated placeholder screens surviving a "complete" label
- sprint reviews that do not produce next-step actions
- tests that happen too late to shape the sprint
- lessons that remain trapped in one review file instead of improving the workflow

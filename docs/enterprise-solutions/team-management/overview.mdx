## Managing Members

This overview page includes practical guidance for adding, editing, and removing members from your enterprise dashboard. For detailed information about user roles and what each role can access, see the Roles and Permissions section above.

### Key Member Management Tasks

- **Adding New Members**: Invite users to your organization with appropriate roles
- **Editing Member Permissions**: Modify user roles and access levels as needed  
- **Removing Members**: Safely remove users while preserving audit trails
- **Bulk Operations**: Manage multiple users efficiently through batch operations
